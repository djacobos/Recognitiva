Welcome to Recognitiva

As an AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate I know that there is not enough time to keep up with rapid evolving technology that we desire to learn, especially if you are a busy person like me. If you have all the time, then its time to get a life! I can't stay behind a screen all day, all night or pay hundreds to attend classroom training. There has to be a better way to take advantage of my time so I decided this is the way and the way to develop my skills is to teach what I learn and Learn and teach.

A new study in Applied Cognitive Psychology researchers led by Aloysius Wei Lun Koh set out to test their theory that teaching improves the teacher’s learning because it compels the teacher to retrieve what they’ve previously studied.

Most of my teaching will be with AWS Rekognition showing recognition and analysis for facial, video and text. I been working with AWS since 2013, so yes I am biased.
